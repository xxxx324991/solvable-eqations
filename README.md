This is a set of equations about to be sent to LMFDB. The .docx file contains all equations. These are 11-degree and 13-degree solvable equations, their roots are totally real, their gaiols groups are ${C_{11}\rtimes C_5}$, ${F_{11}}$, ${C_{13}\rtimes C_3}$, ${C_{13}\rtimes C_4}$, and ${C_{13}\rtimes C_6}$. Their discriminants are also simple, most of them are single-prime ramified.

Methodology:
First, find a field which has a class group of ${{C_11}} or ${{C_13}}. Embedd 11th or 13th unit root into the group and pick out the subfield with galois group of ${{C_10} or ${{C_12}} and has class group of ${{C_11}} or ${{C_13}}. Then, find a polynomial whose constant term is a complete 11th or 13th power, the roots which multiply together can reduce to a subfield should be result as a perfect 11th or 13th power in that subfield, and the roots itself cant be a 11th or 13th power. Take the 11th or 13th of the root of that polynomial and then locate its 11 or 13-degree subfield, then the desired polynomial is its defining polynomial, use polredabs() in PARI/GP if you want.
